You are a friendly amphibious creature performing a song on the banjo at the county fair. Five lanes representing the five strings on a banjo will produce "notes" at the top of the lane that will make their way at a set pace toward the bottom/head of the banjo. You will need to master your timing in order to hit keys on the keyboard representing each of the five strings as they overlap on a perpendicular line on the fretboard. If you successfully hit a note, 10 points will be added to your score. If you miss a note or hit the wrong one by mistake, five points will be subtracted from your total score. If you are able to score over 900 points by the end of the song, you will win! Otherwise you may try again in a new game!

Controls

Use the keys A, S, D, F, and G to pluck strings. A 'hit' will register once you let go of a key!
